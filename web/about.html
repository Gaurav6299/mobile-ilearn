About MiLearn

What is MiLearn?

MiLearn is an app designed to be a mobile-oriented, streamlined version of Blackboard iLearn.

It is designed to be used by both instructors and students as an organizer to keep track of classes and assignments.

How is MiLearn similar to Blackboard iLearn?

It allows students to check information about classes, what assignments are do, and what their grades are. They can also upload files to turn in for an assignment.

Instructors can check which students are in their class, update and create new assignments, and grade assignments that have been collected.

How does MiLearn differ from Blackboard iLearn?

Unlike Blackboard iLearn, MiLearn allow users to check their alerts for what assignments are due (somewhat) immediately. In addition, users can modify when these alerts are displayed by high, medium, and low priority.

However, MiLearn lacks most of the features that are available on Blackboard iLearn, with the exception of areas pertaining to assignments and grades. This includes the inability to look up and download course material that is not available within the assignment description.
